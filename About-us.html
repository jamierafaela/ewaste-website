    Who we are?

    E-Waste Philippines is a dedicated waste management company based in Las Pi√±as, Philippines, committed to sustainable e-waste solutions. Founded in 2023, we aim to tackle the growing issue of electronic waste by purchasing e-waste items from individuals and businesses who might otherwise discard them improperly. We specialize in the collection and responsible disposal of items like computers, phones, televisions, and other electronic devices. Through recycling and reusing, we contribute to reducing the harmful impact of e-waste on our environment and health.

    Mission

    Our vision is a cleaner, safer Philippines where e-waste is no longer a threat to our health or environment. We envision a society that actively participates in sustainable waste management, where every individual recognizes the importance of proper e-waste disposal. By leading this movement, E-Waste Philippines aims to inspire positive environmental change and promote a healthier future for all.

    Vision

    Our mission is to transform how the Philippines manages electronic waste. We strive to promote responsible e-waste disposal by purchasing discarded electronics and recycling or reusing them whenever possible. Through our efforts, we aim to protect our environment and safeguard public health by preventing hazardous toxins, like mercury and lead, from polluting our surroundings.
